📞 Simple invitation — personalise it, keep it human
Hi [Name], hope you're well. Quick favour if that's okay. I've started something new and I need a bit of help with my training. I just need 20 minutes or so to show a few people what I'm doing. Would you be up for helping me out?
✅ If they say yes
Brilliant, thank you. How soon could we jump on a quick call?
🤔 If they ask what it is — or say "send me the info"
I don't want to mess it up trying to explain over the phone — that's why I need to practise showing people properly. How soon could you give me 20 minutes?
💡 Use by phone or text — both work. The goal is to book the conversation, not to explain UW.

First meeting flow 15–20 minutes
# Main focus What to do
1Set the toneOpen with the non-negotiable goal — help them earn money within 48 hours.
2Why they joinedGet the emotional reason, not just the logic. Keep asking what the money changes for them.
3Money goalAgree the first 30-day target. Explain that £1,500–£3,000 is possible in month one when the Fast Start is followed.
4Bigger goalPosition Team Leader simply. Mention the first 6 customers as the early priority milestone on the way.
5First namesHelp them choose 3–5 easy warm contacts. Include homeowners where possible for quick money and early belief.
6Invitation & actionUse the script on page 2, personalise it and agree when they will do the calls or messages that day.
7Supported appointmentsBook the first supported appointments inside 48 hours and explain watch, present, lead briefly.
8Finish adminAgree daily contact where appropriate, set up the PowerPod and make sure Getting Started training is done within 24 hours.
Week one follow-up plan
Timing Main focus What to do
Day 1Fast Start & immediate actionGet page 1 done, add more names, send invitations, book and ideally do the first supported appointments. Mention Getting Started training.
Day 2Check movementCall them. Coach on replies, keep the money link central and make sure appointments are still moving forward.
Days 3–4More activityBuild the list, keep conversations going and continue to support the first appointments. Keep the focus on quick wins, not perfect wording.
Days 5–7Belief & rhythmKeep contact going. Once activity is underway, plug them into the next Team RISE Zoom and live events.
Day 7 reviewReset the next targetReview appointments, customers, confidence and momentum. Map the next simple plan and reinforce the path to first 6 customers and then Team Leader.
